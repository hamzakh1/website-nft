Meet the DigiKawais

DigiKawai is an nft collection of tsundere girls. Its main goal is to build the first ever decentralised dating app which will use the blockchain to help you find the love of your life. First NFT drops will be pictures then the following drop will consist of motion and the final drop will have motion and music. NFT hodlers will have 35% of all profits generated by the dating app. Hodlers will also recieve tons of airdrops like free NFTs,crypto tokens and even a copy of the visual novel. DigiKawai is the gift that keeps on giving !
